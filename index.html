Why use GPS Passes?

A GPS Pass is equivalent to the non GPS version, but at a lower price for a group rate. For the passes which have a GPS version, they can and do sell out separate from the regular version. Buy early to avoid sellout as well as to avoid price increases as the season approaches.
This group is started with the goal to get more people to take advantage of these passes. Tell your friends and family to use a GPS club code like SKICLUB GPS to save money on your pass.
Also follow us on Facebook to keep updated on pass availability
