Why use GPS Passes?

A Wachusett GPS Pass is equivalent to the non GPS version, but at a lower price for a group rate. For the passes which have a GPS version, they can and do sell out separate from the regular version. Buy early to avoid sellout as well as to avoid price increases as the season approaches. This season the price increases by $100 on June 1st.

This group is started with the goal to get more people to take advantage of these passes. Tell your friends and family to use the GPS club code SKICLUB to save money on your pass.

Also follow us on Facebook to keep updated on Wachusett Mountain pass availability
